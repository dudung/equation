# matrix of ones
It is a matrix where every entry or matrix element is equal to one, e.g.

$$
\mathbf{J}_4 = \left[
\begin{array}{cccc}
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \newline
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \newline
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \newline
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 
\end{array}
\right]
$$

and

$$
\mathbf{J}_{3,5} = \left[
\begin{array}{ccccc}
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \newline
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \newline
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 & 1
\end{array}
\right]
$$



where the subscript is only one number for square matrix and two number for non-square matrix.
